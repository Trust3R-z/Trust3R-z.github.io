Suggested production recipe (per clip):
  1. Predict a pointmap pair with MASt3R baseline AND Trust3R on the same image pair.
  2. Render two synchronised Open3D / Polyscope orbits (one per method),
     coloring each point by per-pixel uncertainty (turbo or viridis).
  3. Stack the two orbits side-by-side at 1920x540 (or stacked 960x1080),
     export 30 fps, encode with `ffmpeg -c:v libx264 -crf 22 -pix_fmt yuv420p -an`.
